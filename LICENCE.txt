BackMeUp Core
The BackMeUp Core is the central part of the BackMeUp suite managing and
scheduling backup jobs and providing interfaces to clients and graphical user
interfaces (e.g. BackMeUp Web-Frontend) via a REST API, to BackMeUp Keyserver
for storing authentication data and to plugins for datasources/-sinks and
job-actions.
